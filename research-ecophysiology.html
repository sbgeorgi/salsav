Ecophysiology in Agrivoltaics Systems

Research Questions

This research area focuses on the micro-level interactions between plants, soil, and the altered environment created by solar panels. We investigate fundamental questions about how partial shade and changed microclimates affect plant physiology, water use efficiency, and overall crop health and productivity. How do plants adapt their photosynthetic processes? How much water can be saved? Which crops benefit the most from these conditions?
